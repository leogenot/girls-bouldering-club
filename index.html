Founded in 2025 in Copenhagen, the Girls Bouldering Club creates spaces where women can connect and progress together. Open to all levels, the community is built to offer a supportive environment where women can discover the sport and meet climbing partners. Beyond climbing, it's a place to share experiences and thrive together.
